About

— August 10, 2020

This blog was created and is maintained by Samuel David Gamboa-Tuz. Here, you’ll find tutorials, small projects, reviews of concepts, comments, experiences, and other types of posts related to bioinformatics, data science, and biology.

Samuel is a Mexican biologist and researcher interested in molecular evolution, genomics, bioinformatics, and data science. He got his BSc degree at the Autonomous University of Yucatan and his MSc and PhD degrees at the Yucatan Center for Scientific Research in Mexico. If you are interested in Samuel’s research, please visit his academic website.
